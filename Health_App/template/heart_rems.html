Heart Symptoms:

Chest Pain (Angina)

It is described a crushing pain or heaviness in the center of the chest with radiation of the pain to the arm (usually the left) or jaw.

difficulty breathing, fatigue and light headedness

Women, people who have diabetes, and the elderly may have different pain perceptions and may complain of overwhelming fatigue and weakness or a change in their ability to perform routine daily activities like walking, climbing steps, or doing household chores.

swelling due to fluid retention, or edema
